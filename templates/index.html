Song Ranking

Stereotypy — rank from most stereotyped (highly consistent syllable sequence, few variations) to most variable (many different syllable types, irregular order).
Repeat Propensity — rank from most repetitive (same syllable repeated many times in a row) to least repetitive (each syllable appears only once or twice).
How it works:
You will see spectrograms per round. Click a spectrogram to play its audio. Drag the cards into order — leftmost = most of the trait, rightmost = least. Submit when ready. You can stop at any time; your progress is saved after each round.
